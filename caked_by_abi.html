~ The heart (and hands!) behind CAKED ~

I started this at-home baking business with a love for creativity, community, and cake. I'm turning my lifelong passion into custom creations that are just as special as the moments they're made for.

At CAKED, I believe beautiful, delicious cakes shouldn't break the bank. That's why I focus on making custom cakes that are both affordable and made with care. Giving is at the heart of everything I do, and I'm excited to bring a little more joy one cake at a time.
